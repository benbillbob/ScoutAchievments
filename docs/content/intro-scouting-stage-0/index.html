Helpful tips

You don’t have to do this all at once, and don’t worry - it’s not a test!

You might also do your Introduction to the Scout Section at the same time.

Have a read through here before you have the discussion - then you can be ready to ask any questions you might have too!

Once you’ve finished both your ‘Introduction’ discussions, you’ll be welcomed into Scouting in a special ceremony called an ‘Investiture’. This marks the beginning of your Scouting journey. You’ll be given some badges for your uniform at your ceremony.

Review>

How to get started

Every new Scout will complete an Introduction to Scouting.

Discuss the topics with your Unit Leader, Patrol Leader or adult Leader and track your progress here. They’ll sign you off once you’ve completed each part of the discussion.

This will help you understand who we are as Scouts, and about the adventures to come.
