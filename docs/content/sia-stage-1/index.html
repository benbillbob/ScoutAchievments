    Special Interest Areas

    Special Interest Areas cover six categories that you may choose to explore. What are your interests? Set your own goals and then Plan>Do>Review> to complete a project!

    Helpful tips

    Complete 6 projects, each totalling at least 8 hours across at least 3 areas to achieve this requirement for your Australian Scout Award.

    ####The Special Interest Areas are:

    • Adventure & Sport
    • Arts & Literature
    • Creating a Better World
    • Environment
    • Growth & Development
    • STEM & Innovation
